Program Phases

Our 4-month IT Training Program focuses on building strong technical foundations through hands-on projects, while also emphasizing essential behavioral skills to foster discipline and professionalism for long-term career success.

IT Foundational Training

WEEK (1-2)
  • Covers essential IT basics, building a strong understanding of core concepts.
  • Focuses on hands-on exercises with a structured delivery model.
  • Prepares trainees to work effectively in an agile environment through real-world scenarios.

Specialization

WEEK (3-4)
  • Provides in-depth knowledge and skills in a chosen IT field, such as Front-end development, Back-end development, DevOps, Data Analytics, cloud services.
  • Encourages mastery of specialized tools and technologies relevant to the field.
  • Emphasizes real-world applications within an agile framework to simulate industry environments.

Project Work

WEEK (5-10)
  • Involves hands-on experience with live projects, applying learned skills in real-world scenarios.
  • Encourages collaborative work in an agile environment, simulating industry practices.
  • Focuses on end-to-end project execution, from planning to deployment.
  • Builds problem-solving abilities and adaptability within a structured delivery model.

Interview Prep

WEEK (11-12)
  • Provides targeted training on interview techniques, resume building, and portfolio creation.
  • Conducts mock interviews and real-time feedback sessions to boost confidence and readiness.
  • Offers insights into industry expectations and guidance on adapting to workplace environments.
  • Supports trainees in job placements by aligning skills with employer needs.

An Effective Approach to Launch Your Tech Career

In-Person Classes

Experience hands-on learning in an interactive environment with expert instructors, offering personalized guidance and real-time collaboration to enhance your IT skills effectively.

Mentorship

Unlock your potential with the right mentorship from IT industry professionals. Receive regular performance feedback, career insights, and support tailored to your unique learning journey and goals.

Small Batch of Aspirants

Learn in a focused, collaborative setting with a limited group of peers, ensuring personalized attention, active participation, and stronger connections with mentors and fellow trainees.

Take One Step at a Time

Before diving into project work, we emphasize building a strong foundation in key IT concepts, equipping candidates with essential skills needed to excel in their careers and confidently tackle real-world challenges.

Hands-on Training on Latest Technology

Get practical experience with cutting-edge tools and technologies, mastering real-world applications through interactive sessions designed to prepare you for the fast-evolving IT industry.

Build Solid Portfolio

Create a standout portfolio with real-world projects hosted on GitHub, showcasing your skills and expertise to potential employers, helping you shine in the competitive IT job market.

Assessment based scoring

Track your progress with regular assessments that provide clear scoring metrics, ensuring you understand your strengths and areas for improvement throughout your IT training journey.

Job Placement Guidance

Receive tailored coaching sessions focused on interview preparation, enhancing your confidence and skills to impress potential employers and secure your dream IT job.

Credentialing Your Expertise

Upon completion, every student receives a certificate showcasing their skills and project contributions, which can be shared on LinkedIn or other platforms to enhance networking opportunities.

Motivational Rewards

Enjoy a range of incentives throughout the program, including fee refunds for top performers: 50% for 1st rank, 30% for 2nd rank, and 15% for 3rd rank. These rewards help keep you motivated and engaged in your IT training.

Assured Referrals

We will assured job referrals to the students who maintain a CGPA of 8 or higher receive dedicated support and resources to secure job opportunities in the IT industry, empowering them to launch successful careers.

Flexible Payment

We offer flexible payment methods, including a one-time lump sum payment or the option to pay in installments on a monthly basis. This ensures that you can choose the payment plan that works best for you.
