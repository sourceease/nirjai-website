To support this vision, we developed an in-house web application and a mobile app, Urjobmart.

Throughout this enriching experience, we've gained valuable insights into the IT industry and its evolving demands. Our aspirations have only grown, leading us to actively monitor market trends and specific requirements. This inspired us to introduce our new initiative: an IT Training Program designed under a fresh identity.
